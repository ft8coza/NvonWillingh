Our Story

About Nell von Willingh

As a single mother of two, life has presented its fair share of challenges and triumphs. These experiences have imparted invaluable lessons in patience and resilience, equipping me to confront and conquer every obstacle life has thrown my way. Guided by a higher power, I have cultivated a deep appreciation for the daily struggles that encompass all facets of my existence.

These personal trials have fueled my unwavering determination to help every couple or client realize their dream of celebrating their union or event in alignment with their unique vision. Throughout the years, I have honed a diverse skill set, each facet contributing to my rich tapestry of creativity. My time in the retail industry instilled in me the art of effective communication, patience, and, most importantly, the understanding that everyone carries their own narrative.

This invaluable insight informs my approach when working with clients, recognizing that their ultimate desire is to craft a wedding or event that authentically tells their story. Getting to know you and grasping your vision stands as my paramount objective. I am committed to delivering a tailor-made service because I understand that this journey is deeply intimate and personal.

Drawing inspiration from various creative pursuits such as drawing, art, fashion design, dressmaking, and hairdressing, I apply these artistic skills to benefit my clients profoundly. I view every facet of the planning process through a creative lens, using these skills to guide and support brides and grooms in making decisions that best reflect their special day.

My dedication to each individual couple guarantees that I am by your side every step of the way. I thrive on turning your dreams into reality, making it happen for you.
